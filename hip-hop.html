About Hip Hop Dance

Hip hop dance is a style of dance that evolved as part of hip hop culture. It includes a wide range of styles primarily breaking, locking, and popping, which were created in the 1970s and made popular by dance crews in the United States. Hip hop dance is often improvisational and freestyle in nature.
